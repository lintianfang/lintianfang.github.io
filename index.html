About Me

I am a research assistant in the chair of computer graphics and visualization, TU Dresden, Germany. I am also working in the excellence cluster of Centre for Tactile Internet with Human-in-the-Loop. My research is focusing on Immersive 3D Scanning, Geometry Processing, Virtual Reality.
